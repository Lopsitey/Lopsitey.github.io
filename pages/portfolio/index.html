Personal

GGJ 2026

This is the game we made for GGJ 2026. It involves throwing spears to defeat enemies and survive. Enemies drop mask powerups which change the spear type used. You also have a hunger meter which makes you massive and multiplies your damage temporarily after killing/eating enough enemies. After the jam we decided to make an improved version as we had a few issues with the original, such as some of the incompleted mechanics and inconsistent AI.

Ballatro

A toon-styled pixel pinball roguelike inspired by Balatro and other genre mashups. It adds powerups, a shop and placeable trinkets to a classic pinball loop, letting you chain together strong builds and adapt your run as the table gets more chaotic.
